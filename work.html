projects

Turtlebot Autonomous Food Pickup/Delivery

This project was to develop a ROS package for a Turtlebot to pick up and deliver requested food. The robot explores an unknown world using Lidar and SLAM node gmapping for robot pose and occupancy grid. It explores and maps the environment by following given rviz waypoints and calculating shortest path solution from either A* or bidirectional rrt. While exploring, the robot uses a pre-trained CNN detector to recognize different food vendors and keeps track of their positions to revisit later.

work experience

Tesla Motors Intern Mechanical Design Engineer

Jun 2020 – Sep 2020

The project I took on at Tesla was to design and prototype a new battery runaway flame mitigation mechanism of Powerwall. While details of the design cannot be discussed, I honed skills in DFM and GD&T by designing, quoting, and delivering manufacturing drawings and assembly instructions of the design to international suppliers and in-house shops for on-time delivery.

After the machined parts were delivered and assembled, I worked on the lab setup with LabView, temperature, pressure sensors, and FTIR gas analyzer to collect critical datapoint for thermal and pressure modeling. I executed the DOE to validate the design and certify for a UL requirement. The results from the DOE proved valuable for structural failure analysis and future product design.

ASML Intern Mechanical Design Engineer

Aug 2016 – Dec 2016, May 2017 – Aug 2017

My project at ASML was centered around optimizing the geometry and positioning of the glass optics in EUV systems. I was able to design a new candidate fixture that utilizes vacuum volume to prevent over-constraints when holding down the optics. I developed and executed test DOEs in the clean room to analyze source of optics drift due to humidity and temperature. I also worked to develop a Matlab GUI that process high volume of client’s optics drift failure data for failure analysis and visualization. At ASML, I was able to hone my skills in DFM, FEA analysis, and data analysis for failure.
